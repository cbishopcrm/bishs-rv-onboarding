4 Access Log-in Page · Enter Password · Fill Info · Wait for Approval ⌄
1

Access Log-in Page

Navigate to your group login page for TheCRM. Look for the "Register Here" option.

2

Enter Registration Password

Enter the new user registration password. Typically, our dealership's password is "GoTheCRM", but some dealerships may have a unique password. Contact your dedicated CRM admin or reach out to our support team for assistance.

3

Fill Out User Information

Provide all the requested details to create your new user account, including your store's area code in the "Desired Direct Connect Area Code" field.

4

Wait for Approval

Your user profile has been successfully created! If you do not receive a confirmation text within 30 minutes, please reach out to your internal team to finalize and approve your access.

Communicating With Customers: The Basics

Effective communication is the foundation of building strong relationships with your customers. With TheCRM, you have a powerful suite of tools to engage with your clients in a professional and personalized manner.

Let's dive into the basics of using these communication features to their fullest potential:

Sending Emails

Email remains one of the most common and versatile ways to reach out to customers. Within TheCRM, you can easily compose and send professional emails, whether it's a follow-up, a special offer, or a request for more information. Remember, Olivia is always there to help you craft a personalized email or simply spell-check what you have written.
