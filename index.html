1. Register for Your CRM Account

Follow these four simple steps to create your user account and get your access approved.

1

Access Log-in Page

Navigate to your group login page for TheCRM. Look for the "Register Here" option.

2

Enter Registration Password

Enter the new user registration password. Typically, our dealership's password is "GoTheCRM". Some dealerships may use a unique password — contact your CRM admin or support team for assistance.

3

Fill Out User Information

Provide all the requested details, including your store's area code in the "Desired Direct Connect Area Code" field.

4

Wait for Approval

Your profile has been created! If you do not receive a confirmation text within 30 minutes, reach out to your internal team to finalize and approve your access.

2. Welcome to TheCRM

Once you're logged in, get familiar with your dashboard and your main working view.

Your MyDay Dashboard

When you first log in, you'll be taken to your personalized dashboard. This is your central hub to manage your sales activities and customer relationships.

SalesDaily Page

Your main view will be the SalesDaily page, providing a comprehensive overview of your sales pipeline, upcoming tasks, and key customer information. This is where you'll spend most of your time.

Note: Your MyDay may look slightly different depending on your dealership configuration.
