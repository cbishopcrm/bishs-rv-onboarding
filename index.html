User Onboarding Guide

Welcome to TheCRM platform, where cutting-edge AI technology meets seamless customer relationship management. Our platform is designed to help you manage customer interactions with unprecedented efficiency and effectiveness. To make your onboarding experience as smooth as possible, we've created a detailed step-by-step guide to help you get started.

Register for Your CRM Account ⬇️
  1. Access Log-in Page — Navigate to your group login page for TheCRM. Look for the "Register Here" option.
  2. Enter Registration Password — Enter the new user registration password. Typically, our dealership's password is "GoTheCRM", but some dealerships may have a unique password. Contact your dedicated CRM admin or reach out to our support team for assistance.
  3. Fill Out User Information — Provide all the requested details to create your new user account, including your store's area code in the "Desired Direct Connect Area Code" field.
  4. Wait for Approval — Your user profile has been successfully created! If you do not receive a confirmation text within 30 minutes, please reach out to your internal team to finalize and approve your access.

Communicating With Customers: The Basics

Effective communication is the foundation of building strong relationships with your customers. With TheCRM, you have a powerful suite of tools to engage with your clients in a professional and personalized manner. Let's dive into the basics of using these communication features to their fullest potential.

Sending Emails

Email remains one of the most common and versatile ways to reach out to customers. Within TheCRM, you can easily compose and send professional emails — whether it's a follow-up, a special offer, or a request for more information. Remember, Olivia is always there to help you craft a personalized email or simply spell-check what you have written.

Sending Texts

SMS is one of the fastest ways to reach customers directly. Send quick updates, appointment reminders, and personalized messages — all from within TheCRM.

Sending Photos, Videos, and Other Multimedia

You can also upload and send videos, images, or other multimedia content to your customers. This can be useful for showcasing products, providing visual instructions, or simply adding a personal touch to your interactions.
