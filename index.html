Register for Your CRM Account

Four quick steps to get your login credentials set up and approved.

4 Show the 4 registration steps ⌄
1

Access the Log-in Page

Navigate to bishsrv.thecrm.com/login and click the "New User? Register Here" link under the Sign In button.

2

Enter the Registration Password

On the New User Registration screen, enter the dealership password. For Bish's RV it's typically "GoTheCRM". If that doesn't work, reach out to your CRM admin. Click Continue.

3

Fill Out Your Profile

Complete your profile — name, mobile phone, work email, and store details. Make sure to enter your store's area code in the "Desired DirectConnect Area Code" field, then set a username and password and click Sign Up.

4

Wait for Approval

Your profile has been created. If you don't receive a confirmation text within 30 minutes, reach out to your internal team to finalize and approve your access.
